Images

IIT Bangalore
The Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore, established in 1909, is India's leading institution for advanced research and education in science and technology. It offers interdisciplinary programs across engineering and natural sciences, renowned for its research output and innovative environment, shaping the future of science and engineering in India.
IIT Kharagpur
Founded in 1951, IIT Kharagpur is India's first IIT and a premier engineering institution. Located in West Bengal, it offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Known for its rigorous academics and extensive research facilities, IIT Kharagpur has produced numerous successful alumni who have excelled globally.
